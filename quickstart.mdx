## What is ParityDeals?

ParityDeals is your operating system for usage-based billing, metering, and monetization. It helps you launch and scale products faster. You can easily deploy your pricing today and iterate with confidence tomorrow.

ParityDeals acts as the single source of truth for all customer usage and spending data. This empowers your teams to:

- Manage and update pricing strategies.
- Create real-time billing experiences.
- Drive revenue-generating workflows.

## Empowering Your Teams

Our platform gives engineering teams precise control over how features are packaged and priced. This allows your product and GTM teams to rapidly tweak, test, and roll out pricing changes without writing any code.


## Core Capabilities

ParityDeals gives you a complete toolkit to monetize your product. After a short integration with your codebase, you can use our no-code platform to:

- **Define Monetization:** Pinpoint exactly which product functionalities you want to charge for.

- **Adapt Packaging and Pricing:** Define and continuously adjust your product plans, and pricing models. This includes freemium, trials, usage-based tiers, and more.

- **Manage Customers:** Handle customer accounts, their subscriptions, and monitor their feature usage in one place.

- **Control Paywalls Remotely:** Instantly update and manage access rules for your paywalls without deploying new code.

- **Tailor Pricing for Any Audience:** Offer customized pricing based on location, purchasing power parity, device, or browser to improve monetization. All changes can be updated remotely without touching any code.
